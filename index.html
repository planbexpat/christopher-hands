About

Present, and findable.

In these times we can be overstimulated by the sheer volume of information with which we interact. These intrusions can make us numb to the point that we may neglect physical signals — warnings to make a change in lifestyle.

I will help you to release past traumas so that you can move on to recognise triggers and create yourself a new map to live more fluidly.

I work between Europe and South & Central America. Recharging through nature and enjoying peaceful moments — along with my family — keep me going.

I use a co-active coaching model that facilitates an alliance between two equals for the purpose of meeting your needs: a person rather than a problem to be solved.

Approach

Five dimensions of a whole life.

The work moves across all 5 dimensions. Some clients have one door in mind and discover other possibilities once they commit to change.

i.

Mind

Self-talk, performance thinking, fantasy, compulsive patterns.

tap to turn

On the mind.

Most coaching treats the mind as the thing to fix — quiet the self-talk, reframe the thoughts. My work treats the mind as one voice in a room that has several. The self-talk isn't the enemy. It's information about whose voice is running the meeting right now.

ii.

Emotions

Feelings from the past, body image, suppression, the capacity for intimacy.

tap to turn

On emotions.

Emotions aren't problems to manage. They're the body's translation of experiences the words haven't caught up to yet. We were taught to rank them — good ones, bad ones, ones we're allowed to have. The practice is learning to listen before deciding what to do with what you're feeling.

tap to turn back
iii.

Body

Your sexual pattern, architecture, function — being a successful lover.

tap to turn

On the body.

Most coaching treats the body as something to manage — fix the sleep, fix the stress. My work treats it as something to listen to. When you can't sleep, the question isn't how do I sleep better. It's what is the sleeplessness trying to say. The body often notices what the mind refuses to.

iv.

Spirit

Peak experiences, sacred sexuality, the inner self, the transcendent.

tap to turn

On spirit.

Spirit in this work doesn't mean religion, and it doesn't require belief. It's the part of you that asks why when your life is outwardly fine but inwardly hollow. The peak experiences you can't explain. Sacred sexuality lives here. So does grief. So does the quiet you sometimes need without knowing why.

v.

Energy

The build-up, containment, and expression — often overlooked.

tap to turn

On energy.

The one most people skip, because no one taught them to notice it. Energy is how much of you is actually here right now, versus how much is spent rehearsing yesterday or defending against tomorrow. You can't think your way into more of it. You can only learn where yours goes.
